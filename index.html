MORAL DIMENSION

WHY CHOSE THE TITLE MORAL DIMENSION

Moral dimension in the book revolves around the
moral of embracing resilience and strength in facing
apartheid and inequality. The importance of love,
family and community are the sources of support in facing challenges.
